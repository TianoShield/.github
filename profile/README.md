## Hi there 👋

We are TianoShield Group!

The goal of TianoShield is to secure the Tianocore software ecosystem. 

TianoShield aims to enhance the security posture of the TianoCore ecosystem and improve its overall open-source development process and practices.

Our group consists of faculty and students from the University of Colorado Colorado Springs (UCCS) and Purdue University. 

We also collaborate with industry partners such as Intel, Binarly, GitHub Security, and ARM.

Visit our website - https://tianoshield.github.io/home to learn more about us.
